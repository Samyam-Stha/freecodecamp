Mathhew Perry

  • Matthew Langford Perry was born on August 19, 1969, in Williamstown, Massachusetts, USA.
  • His parents are Suzanne Marie Morrison and John Bennett Perry, an actor.
  • Perry gained widespread fame for his role as Chandler Bing on the popular television sitcom "Friends," which aired from 1994 to 2004.
  • His portrayal of Chandler earned him critical acclaim and several award nominations.
  • Perry has appeared in various films, including "Fools Rush In" (1997), "The Whole Nine Yards" (2000), and its sequel "The Whole Ten Yards" (2004).
  • He has also had a successful career in theater, starring in productions such as "The End of Longing" and "The Odd Couple."
  • Perry has been open about his struggles with alcohol and prescription drug addiction during his time on "Friends" and in the years following the show's conclusion.
  • After "Friends," Perry worked on several TV shows, including "Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip" and "The Odd Couple."
  • He has also appeared in guest roles on various series.
  • Perry has been romantically linked to several high-profile personalities.
  • He has been involved in philanthropy, supporting causes related to cancer research and treatment.
  • In 2021, Perry and the rest of the "Friends" cast participated in a reunion special, which garnered significant attention and nostalgia from fans.
